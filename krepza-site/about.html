About us

Making grocery prices clearer in Lithuania.

Krepza is being built to help shoppers compare everyday grocery prices across Lithuanian retailers before they choose where to buy.

What we are building

Our goal is to turn live market data into a simple product view: product prices, basket totals, store coverage, and useful deal signals for cities including Vilnius and Kaunas.

Why it matters

Grocery prices change often. Krepza gives shoppers a faster way to see whether a product is cheaper elsewhere and whether a weekly basket is worth splitting across stores.
